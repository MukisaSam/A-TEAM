Word From the Head Teacher

Dear Viewers, Readers,

I am excited to welcome you to the Year 2025! Praise be to God and Happy New Year!
Mengo Senior School is a vibrant community committed to providing quality education through practical skills, teamwork, self-reliance and God fearing persons.
Through Fearing God, Respect of Persons and Property and Integrity, we always strive to be at the helm of producing competent human resource to serve the Church, the State and the World.
This year as we celebrate 130 years of existence, we are working with Ephesians 2:10, as God’s workmanship built for greater works.
We will continue to have engaging and challenging academic programs, exciting co-curricular activities, opportunities for personal growth and development and a strong focus on student well-being and mental health.

We welcome you to partner with us as we make Mengo bigger and greater by the grace of God. I trust that 2025 will be an exciting journey with greater aspirations and achievements. In our strategic plan is a more comprehensive DIT School in which we shall have even more sophisticated skilling for our learners. We look forward to improved technology and innovations, greater achievements, opportunities and a renewed sense of purpose. A bigger e-learning Library pointing us to our virtual school is top on agenda. Why not partner with us as we embark on the New Dining Hall Project, Swimming pool and many others. As a school we seek to work better, get bigger and serve you better with explicit systems.
Stay up-to-date with our latest news, events, and announcements from by visiting our website regularly, following us on our social media handles, walking into our Public Relations Office to have a chat, joining our Donor’s Club and praying for us. The New Year is set before us with blank pages, let us write upon each day’s page with determination and exceptional resolve, that when we look back it shall be with rejoicing.
Let's make this year amazing!
I look forward to a fantastic year ahead, a year filled with greater blessings and an un matched show of God’s glory. I cannot wait to see us all achieve greater things.
May God Bless You All!
Akwana Akira Ayomba!
Nantagya Grace Ssebanakitta.
