Admissions Information

Join Mengo Senior School and be part of a legacy of excellence.

How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for admission:

  1. Download the application form from our website or pick it up from the school office.
  2. Fill out the form completely and attach the required documents.
  3. Submit the form to the admissions office before the deadline.
  4. Attend the scheduled interview or entrance exam.
  5. Wait for the admission decision, which will be communicated via email or phone.

Admission Requirements

  • Completed application form
  • Copy of birth certificate
  • Previous school report cards
  • Two passport-sized photos
  • Application fee receipt
